Problem Statement

The given problem statement of our project is to develop a web app that issues tenders for pharmaceutical products online and further make the best deal.

Online Tender Management System will enable the suppliers to get all the tender details online and provide the facility to bid on particular tenders.

Objective

Tender Management software solution is an online service that helps suppliers and buyers to interact with each other to achieve prominent efficiency in all aspects of a tendering process. Whether it is complex tendering or a simple process, Tender Management helps an organization in executing an effective e-procurement strategy and makes tendering function hassle-free.

The web application provides an interface between medicine manufacturers and hospital officials through an online link.

Modular Design

There are two main modules Hospital and Manufacturer module:

  • Admin or Hospital module: Admin can upload tenders-related information to the website and delete old tender details. She can update the latest data on the website. The basic functionalities that hospital officials get are issuing, deleting, editing, viewing history, and selecting the final bid for the tender.
  • Manufacturer module: The user who registers with the application will get a unique username and password to look after available tenders and download related files and apply for tenders and can bid tenders online. Manufacturers on the other hand have functionalities such as the bid for tenders, view tenders, etc. Apart from these other options include getting notifications and updating your account.
