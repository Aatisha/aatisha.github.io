Introduction

Global warming is impacting the earth in ways we cannot imagine. The growing concerns are not to be taken lightly. This is something that needs serious attention not just by governments but by each individual too.

Reading articles about climate change and watching news makes people wonder what they can do about it. Considering the fact that not everyone has the same resources, opportunities and abilities to make contributions, but the fact that the majority of the population uses digital platforms in their daily lives, the platform itself could be used to encourage people to make simple contributions.

UX for Sustainability

The core idea of the application is to make people make sustainable and eco-friendly life choices through setting up individual goals or with a partner and make the process of achieving these goals accountable and yet fun.
