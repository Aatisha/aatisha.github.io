Problem Statement

The aim was to develop a model which could monitor the health of the plants by using the Internet of Things (IoT). The health of the plant is measured by monitoring the following environmental parameters:

Soil Moisture

Temperature

Humidity

Overview

The project describes a low-cost Arduino Uno-based experimental model which is a highly efficient module for calculating environmental parameters for plant health monitoring.

The parameter values are analyzed with the normal condition values.

The status of plant health is visually displayed by the LEDs and the serial monitor of the Arduino IDE i.e. Arduino Sketch.

The Process

How did I reach here?

It started with understanding the problem and designing the system implementation.

System architecture

The architecture of the model is divided into three modules. They are as follows:

  • Sensing Unit: Composed of two sensors, temperature and humidity, and soil moisture sensors. They collect the environmental readings and pass them on to the processing unit.
  • Processing Unit: Composed of an Arduino Uno board which processes the readings and compares them with the normal threshold values through embedded C.
  • Display Unit: Composed of a serial monitor along with LEDsto indicated the status of plants quantitatively. Green LED blinks for normal plant health and red for abnormal readings.

Working principle

The given activity diagram displays the flow of the working system from fetching the sensor values to displaying it as visual information. The monitoring helps the plant owners take proper care of their plants.

Future work

What is the future scope of the project?

In the future, we plan to enhance our model with more functionalities. These will include the following:

  • The number of LEDs will be increased to specifically find out which parameter is not satisfactory. Therefore, there will be more different colored LEDs each for different output.
  • Next, we plan to store the values of the sensors with date and time. For this, there is a need for creating a database. This can be used to monitor the health of plants on a large scale.
  • Something related to mobile control and on click irrigation
