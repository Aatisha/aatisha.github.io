A little bit about me...

I am a Product Designer with a background in software and data, bridging research, design, and front-end development to craft intuitive and impactful digital experiences.

My interests lie in designing collaborative web-based systems, conducting usability studies, and building prototypes that are both user-centered and technically sound. I've led projects across e-commerce, scientific data visualization, and sustainability, applying Figma, Angular, HTML/CSS, and Agile workflows. Lately, I’ve been enjoying the creative rhythm of vibe coding.

Currently, I'm designing a conversational AI video sales agent at MyWorkerAI, focused on scalable solutions for both B2B and B2C platforms.

I design with the hope of creating impact — even if just a little.
