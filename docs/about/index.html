Hey there! I'm Aatisha - a Software Engineer working in the field of data engineering, looking to explore my horizons in user experience and design.

I was born and raised in the northern state of Bihar in India. Growing up, I had always been fascinated by the advancements in technology as it has the power to unite people irrespective of their social, economic, geographical, or racial differences.

I did my bachelor's in Information Technology from Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology, Chennai, India. I found my passion for design during my undergrad years when I contributed to design activities for various college events and products. It truly makes me happy, and my goal is to be able to solve user pain points through inclusive and assistive user interaction designs.

I am a Software Engineer at HashedIn by Deloitte in Bangalore, India. I develop data delivery pipelines for our clients with Python, Airflow, and various GCP services. I also trained college graduates in visualization tools as part of their onboarding journey.

Before that, I was an ETL Developer at DXC Technology, Bangalore, where I worked on services like AWS, Informatica Cloud, and IBM Datastage. In 2019, I also completed a 4-month internship at Cognizant Technology Solutions, Chennai, to upskill my practical knowledge of advanced JAVA and PSQL.

Outside of work, I am an avid reader. I read to find an escape to explore life narratives through someone else's perspectives. It helps me empathize with the world on a much deeper and more personal level.

I contribute towards mother nature by planting trees every month to combat climate change and plan to work more in helping to create awareness about the same. I also indulge in photography and playing guitar and keyboard.

It would be a pleasure to collaborate on ideas, so let's connect!
